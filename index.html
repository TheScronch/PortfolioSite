GAMES
WIZARD GAME
A card collecting real-time strategy. Traverse a strange, interdimensional world while collecting elemental cards to cast spells against the evil that lurks.

Role: Lead Designer, Lead Programmer, 2D Artist.
KITTY HAWK
An endless, flying VR simulator inspired by 80's era futuristic imagery. Navigate a variety of obstacles while collecting coins to earn rewards and a competitive high score.

Currently in early access on Steam.

Roles: Obstacle Designer, Enemy AI Programmer, 3D Artist.
PUFF PUFF
An arcade style platformer where players take control of a puffy cloud. Players must keep the cloud airbourne by firing lightning into the ground, but avoid striking innocent NPCs at all costs.

Developed in 2 days for PixelFest's 2017 Game Jam in Norfolk, VA

Role: Lead Designer, Lead Programmer, 2D Artist.
NEKO NEKO VILLAGE
Build a shrine to the phantom god Anuk to avoid a daily, deathly sacrifice. Collect necessary survival resources for your villagers to protect them from the harsh, wintery elements.

Made in 2 days for the 2016 Global Game Jam.

Role: Designer, 2D Artist, 2D Animator.
